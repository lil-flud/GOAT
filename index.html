The Greatest Live Performance You'll Ever Experience!

Dave Matthews Band is the most talented collection of musicians you will find in the Jam Band scene. No two live shows are alike, every time you hear them play a particular song it is like hearing them play it for the first time. They have had many accomplishments in their career: 14 grammy nominations with one resulting in a win, ranked in the Top 100 Most Sold Albums in the USA at 31 million sold, 5 Top Selling Albums, multiple Rock Hall Of Fame Nominations, and many more! While the have a very accomplished studio career, their live performances are what really makes them stand out. Their heavy emphasis on improvisation and deep roster of songs makes every show feel as like a brand new experience. Dave Matthews

The man himself. Aside from being the founder of the band he plays a few roles: lead singer, main songwriter, and rhythym guitarist. Dave was born in South Africa and went on to live all around the world throughout his life, which is often cited as a large reason for why the band has such a "worldly" sound. Inspiration was taken from a lot of different places when he was living abroad. For example; their song Minarets is remniscient of middle eastern music.
